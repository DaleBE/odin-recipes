Mushroom and Berry Gnome Tarts

Here's a delightful dish that will win you favour with the Gnomes that live near you. Gnomes are amusing little creatures and can be quite helpful to humans if they are on your side. They are also good fun for cats to play with and it would suit you well to summon some around for us cats. Though these can be made at anytime, I would recommend making these on a clear autumn evening as the sun is setting.

Ingredients

  • fresh pastry dough
  • woodland berries
  • crushed roasted hazelnuts
  • tart-sized woodland mushroom caps
  • fresh Thymus vulgarus (Thyme)

Methodology

  1. Take the fresh pastry dough, roll it out and cut it into disks and fit them into small tart tins.
  2. Take the berries and put a small layer of them into the bottoms of each of the tart shells.
  3. Next place the mushroom caps gill side up into each tart shell and finish them off by sprinkling a little fresh Thyme and crushed hazelnuts on top
  4. Bake these all until they look just lovely and cooked
  5. Take the just baked tarts outside quickly and place them at the base of a tree or rocky outcrop, preferably near a woodland.
  6. And there you have it! You are finished.
